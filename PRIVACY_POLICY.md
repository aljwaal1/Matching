عند تصدير تقرير أو إرسال ملاحظة بالبريد، لا تغادر البيانات الجهاز إلا عندما يختار المستخدم بنفسه حفظها أو مشاركتها عبر تطبيق آخر.

يعرض التطبيق إعلان Banner من **Google AdMob** في الصفحة الرئيسية، ويستخدم منصة موافقة المستخدم من Google عند الحاجة. وفق إفصاحات Google Mobile Ads SDK، قد تجمع Google وتشارك تلقائيًا:

- معرّفات الجهاز أو الحساب، بما فيها معرّف الإعلانات عند توفره.

تُستخدم هذه البيانات لأغراض عرض الإعلانات والتحليلات ومنع الاحتيال والأمان، وتُنقل بصورة مشفرة. يمكن للمستخدم تعديل خيارات خصوصية الإعلانات عندما يظهر هذا الخيار داخل التطبيق، كما يمكنه إعادة ضبط معرّف الإعلانات أو حذفه من إعدادات Android.

# Privacy Policy — Account Matching

**Effective date: July 23, 2026**

**Account Matching** (`com.explapp.accountmatching`) helps users match customer and supplier statements, prepare bank reconciliations, and export reports.

## Data processed locally

The app processes files selected by the user, including Excel, CSV, and PDF files, together with balances, reconciliation items, matching results, and exported reports. This processing occurs on the user's device. The developer does not send the contents of user files, balances, or reports to developer-operated servers.

Archive results and app settings are stored locally on the device. Users can delete them from within the app, by clearing app data, or by uninstalling the app.

When exporting a report or sending feedback by email, data leaves the device only when the user chooses to save or share it through another app.

## Advertising and data handled by Google

The app displays a Google AdMob banner on the home screen and uses Google's consent platform where required. According to the Google Mobile Ads SDK disclosure, Google may automatically collect and share:

- IP address, which may be used to estimate approximate location.
- User interactions with the app and advertisements.
- Diagnostic and performance information.
- Device or account identifiers, including the advertising ID when available.

This data is used for advertising, analytics, fraud prevention, and security, and is encrypted in transit. Users can manage advertising privacy options when available in the app and can reset or delete the advertising ID through Android settings.

## Accounts and financial transactions

The app does not require account creation, and the developer does not sell user data. The app does not connect directly to bank accounts and does not provide payments, money transfers, loans, or trading services.

## Children

The app is a professional accounting tool and is not directed to children.
